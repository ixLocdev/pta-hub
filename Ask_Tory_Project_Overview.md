# Ask Tory — Project Overview

## What Is It?

**Ask Tory** is a searchable app where you type a question and get an instant answer — like Google, but for your organization's own knowledge.

It was built as a demo for Tory Burch retail associates, giving them quick access to product info, talking points, care instructions, styling tips, and short videos right from their phone or iPad.

The idea works for any organization where people need fast answers — retail, schools, nonprofits, healthcare, etc.

---

## The Problem It Solves

People learn things in training but forget the details when they actually need them. Binders, PDFs, and course materials are hard to search through in the moment.

This app flips that: instead of "memorize everything upfront," it's **"search for what you need, right now."**

---

## What You See When You Open It

### Search

The home screen is just a search bar. Type what you need, and results appear instantly — grouped, ranked by relevance, and easy to scan.

A few smart touches:
- **Understands synonyms** — searching "purse" also finds results for "bag" and "handbag"
- **Knows what you mean** — if you type "how do I care for suede," care guides get pushed to the top
- **Best Answer** — when one result is clearly the best match, it gets highlighted at the top of the page

### 6 Types of Content

| Type | What It Is |
|------|-----------|
| **Spotlight** | In-depth product or topic card |
| **Talking Points** | Ready-to-use scripts (with a copy button) |
| **Video** | Short 15–45 second how-to clips |
| **Compare** | Side-by-side comparison of two items |
| **Care Guide** | Do's and don'ts for materials and maintenance |
| **Styling** | Outfit ideas tied to a specific product |

### Recommendation Wizard

A short 3-question quiz that helps someone who doesn't know what to search for:
1. What's the occasion?
2. How much do you need to carry?
3. What's your style preference?

It then shows the top 3 recommendations ranked by fit (Best Match, Great Match, Also Consider), each with a relevant talking point.

### Browse & Videos

- **Browse** — a catalog view of all products, organized by family
- **Videos** — a feed of all short clips with transcripts

### Admin Dashboard

A behind-the-scenes view for managers, with 4 tabs:

- **Overview** — key stats like total searches, scripts copied, and peak usage times
- **Searches** — what people search for most, plus a **Content Gaps** section that shows terms people searched but got no results for (so you know what content to add next)
- **Content** — which content gets the most views and copies
- **Wizard** — how many people complete the quiz, where they drop off, and what gets recommended most

The Content Gaps feature is especially useful — it tells you exactly what's missing based on what people are actually looking for.

---

## What Makes This Different from a Regular Website

- **Search is the main way to use it** — no menus to dig through
- **Results are ranked by relevance**, not just listed
- **Copy button** on scripts and talking points for immediate use
- **Guided quiz** for people who don't know what to look for
- **Built-in analytics** — every search, click, and copy is tracked so you can see what's actually getting used
- **Content gap detection** — shows you what to create next based on real usage
- **Works on phones and iPads** — designed for touch, can be saved to the home screen like a regular app
- **No expensive hosting required** — it can run for free
